Orquette's Maze Adventure

Once home to a majestic octopus who tragically lost his tentacles in the mysterious mazes scattered throughout the aquarium, Orquette, the aquarium owner, now awaits a hero to restore what was lost.

Solve the mazes, each more challenging than the last. For every maze you solve, you retrieve a lost tentacle. Orquette rewards your efforts with playful and slightly naughty interactions that will leave you eager for more.

Who are we?

the company was born when the lead developer, Flying Salmon, was unemployed and had a lot of free time for leisure. one night, I had a dream and decided to transform it into a video game, "Memeventure Paradox". the story of the game is random. if it makes me laugh, then I implement it. the game world is a form of escapism for me. sometimes, I put myself in the shoes of the characters.

Thanks to the team for putting so much efforts into creating the games.
