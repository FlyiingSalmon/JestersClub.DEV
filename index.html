Orquette's Maze Adventure

Once home to a majestic octopus who tragically lost his tentacles in the mysterious mazes scattered throughout the aquarium, Orquette, the aquarium owner, now awaits a hero to restore what was lost.

Solve the mazes, each more challenging than the last. For every maze you solve, you retrieve a lost tentacle. Orquette rewards your efforts with playful and slightly naughty interactions that will leave you eager for more.
